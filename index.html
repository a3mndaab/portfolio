About Me

I am a highschool sophomore who is dedicated to making an impact on the community. I enjoy service and helping others through things like participating in my schools Labre Ministry, founding my schools's UNICEF Club, and fundraising money for initatives in Africa through my school's African Student Association. I am also dedicated to advocating for better health quality in my community. Through my internship at Emory University Hospital, I learned the importanc of quality healthcare. In the future, I plan on continuing this message and bringing better care to those in my community and around the world.

I live with my mother, father, and brother. I was born in Atlanta, moved to Clarkston, and now I have been living in Stone Mountain for nine years. One of my biggest passions is traveling. I want to visit Dubai, New York, San Francisco, Italy, Hawaii, Japan, Korea, Singapore, and so much more.
